Certification

1. The students needs to clear written and oral examinations held internally & by external professionals to get certified for a given level & get eligible for the next level.

2. In case of failure, the institute can give two more exam chances and required training to clear a given level.

3. The student can continue any phase or level anytime, not necessarily he continues the batch he got admitted.
