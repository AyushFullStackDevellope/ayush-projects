About Orangelo

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Orangelo has been a beacon of culinary excellence in our community. Our passionate team of chefs and staff are dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience that combines innovative flavors with timeless techniques.

We take pride in sourcing the finest local and organic ingredients, supporting sustainable farming practices, and creating dishes that not only please the palate but also nourish the soul.

At Orangelo, we believe that great food has the power to bring people together, create lasting memories, and celebrate life's special moments. Join us for an extraordinary culinary journey that will delight your senses and warm your heart.
